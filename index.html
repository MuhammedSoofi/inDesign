We are situated in Mavoor, Calicut, a town that encapsulates the essence of tradition and progress in Kerala's Kozhikode district. Since the 1960s, Mavoor has undergone significant transformation, particularly with the establishment of the Gwalior Rayons factory, later rebranded as Grasim, which has left an indelible mark on its identity. Mavoor's religious landscape is marked by a rich tapestry of temples, mosques, and churches, reflecting its cultural diversity and unity. Educational institutions such as Mavoor GMUP School and Mahlara Arts & Science College contribute to the town's intellectual vibrancy, while the Mavoor Public Library, housed within the Grama Panchayath Office, stands as a beacon of knowledge and learning. In Mavoor, the past seamlessly intertwines with the present, shaping a narrative of resilience and community cohesion.
